My Story

Hello, I am Hamza ,I studied Intelligent Computer System Engineering at Algiers 1, one of Algeria's top universities. During my studies, I worked on several internships, which helped me build a solid foundation in system design and engineering. Now that I've got a couple of years of work experience, I'm involved in some big e-learning projects where we use artificial intelligence to make learning more personalized and engaging. On the side, I also create content about technology, programming, and AI. I write blog posts, make videos, and generally try to share what I know with others. I enjoy breaking down complicated tech concepts and making them easier to understand. I think technology can make a huge impact on people's lives, and I'm glad I can contribute to that.
